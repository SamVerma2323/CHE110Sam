Take Action



Create a Change

There are a variety of ways a person take a stand against climate change. Whether big or small, you can always help make your community a better place.

Action Checklist

Join the fight! Once you have completed each step, check it off the list!

  • Step 1: Educate yourself on water availability and find out who advocates for it using our "Resources" page.
  • Step 2: Register to vote.
  • Step 3: Write to Congress using this pre-written email.
  • Step 4: Call your representatives.
  • Step 5: Work to reduce your carbon footprint: avoid wasting food & water, limit meat consumption, and focus on using renewable energy.
